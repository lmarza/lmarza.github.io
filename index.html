Luca Marzari

University of Verona, Verona, Italy.

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Verona, advised by Prof. Alessandro Farinelli and Prof. Ferdinando Cicalese . My research focuses on developing efficient and reliable methods for verifying the correctness of DNNs, particularly in the context of DRL applications. I am also exploring the use of approximate verification techniques inside the DRL training process, in order to foster the learning of safer behaviors. For more information on my research activity, you can visit the Research page.
